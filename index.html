Conway's Game of life


by nischal lamichhane
How It Works

this is a zero player game. however, you can set the initial state (first state) of the grid.
rules:
Birth: A dead cell with exactly three live neighbors becomes a live cell in the next generation.
Survival: A live cell with two or three live neighbors remains alive in the next generation.
Death: A live cell with fewer than two live neighbors dies (underpopulation) due to loneliness.
Death: A live cell with more than three live neighbors dies (overpopulation) due to crowding.
